Why is everyone using clouds?

why:

  • Pay for usage
  • Support
  • Resiliency

Pay for usage:

  • Low upfront commitment
  • Low loss if it doesn't work
  • Budget can scale with growth

Support:

  • Someone to call
  • Effectively a bigger team
  • Common patterns and practises

Resiliency:

  • Self healing
  • Globally distributed
  • Quick response times
